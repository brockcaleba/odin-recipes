Chocolate Chip Cookie Cheesecake

Description

A delicious and rich cheesecake with chocolate chip cookie inside.

Ingredients

  • 3 Packages of Cream Cheese (8 oz. each)
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Tsp. Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Rolls Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough (20 oz. each)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Beat eggs.
  3. Mix cream cheese, eggs, granulated sugar and vanilla extract together well.
  4. Slice cookie dough rolls into 1/3 inch slices.
  5. Place slices of one roll into a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
  6. Press together to cover any holes.
  7. Spoon in cream cheese mixture evenly.
  8. Top with remaining slices of cookie dough.
  9. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until golden and center is slightly firm.
  10. Remove from oven and let cool, then refrigerate.
  11. Slice when cool and enjoy!
