Peanut Butter Pie

Description

This recipe is for a peanut butter pie. It is creamy and sweet and goes best on an Oreo crust.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz. Cream Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp. Milk
  • 1 Container of Cool Whip (9 oz)
  • 1 Pie Shell
  • 3/4 Cup Confectioners Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Soften cream cheese
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Add peanut butter
  4. Gently fold in Cool Whip
  5. Let chill for 20-30 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
